About Me

Isaac Sseonyonjo is a reverted Muslim from a Protestant family of five. He was born on 15/10/1988 at Mengo Hospital and is the third child of Nabukenya Mary and the late Ssekayombya Dickson. He is Munyoro by tribe from Kakumiro District (Bugangazi County). His elder brother, Oscar Peter Mbabali (aka Diamond Oscar), is a Ugandan artist. His elder sister Angella Nakyeyune and younger sister Sharon Nazziwa live in the UAE; his youngest sibling Abel Muyanja died in 2016. He started working after secondary school in 2007 in Nabugabo, and in 2009 he joined Kiseka Market as a spare-parts seller, where his business career developed. He is married.
